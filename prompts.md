# Introduction

This document records the AI prompts used during the planning and architecture phase of the TechoVerse project. The prompts were primarily used to understand concepts, explore design ideas, clarify implementation logic, and make informed architectural decisions.

---

# 1. Project Planning

### Prompt

**Purpose**

To select a project with appropriate complexity and scope.

Artificial Intelligence was used as a learning and planning assistant throughout the project.
